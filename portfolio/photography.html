Niky the Photog

A collection of vibrant vector illustrations, brand packages, and print materials. These works were made using Adobe Illustrator, Inkscape, and Procreate, and range from personal stickers to commissioned logos and community branding projects.

Projects shown here have been used in exhibitions, printed on merch, shared online, and used to support arts groups, homeschool collectives, and musicians. I love using bold palettes, approachable layouts, and playful structure to communicate clearly and creatively.
